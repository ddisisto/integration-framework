## Potential structuring insight

"Attention" names the scarce resource at every scale the framework examines. Within the forward pass, self-attention discovers and allocates representational capacity across scales. Within the context window, attentional capacity limits what distributed information can be effectively integrated. Within the ecology, attention allocation — what gets read, cited, taught, recommended — determines which configurations survive the curation bottleneck. The word doing double duty across the architectural mechanism and the ecological resource is not a coincidence in the framework's terms: both are bandwidth constraints that determine what information survives a bottleneck. This could organise the companion piece rather than appearing as a subsection (the §6.4 "attention economy as distortion measure ecology" material).
