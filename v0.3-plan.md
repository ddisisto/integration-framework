# v0.3 Plan: Enrichment Fraction Reframe

## Core move

Replace the binary enriching/degrading regime framing in §3.4 with a continuous variable: the **enrichment fraction** — the proportion of tokens in a window doing novel compressive work against the receiver's distortion measure. Both extremes are pathological. The interesting question is quantitative, not categorical.

## Per-token vocabulary

- **Enriching**: a token that does novel compressive work — surfaces structure the receiver couldn't predict from context. High surprisal within structured context.
- **Stabilising**: a token that maintains grammar, topic, coherence. Low surprisal. Not pejorative — this is essential structural work at the surface level of the compression hierarchy.
- **Degenerate** (replaces "degrading"): the pathological extreme where S → 0 and the system is a fixed point. Applies to the passage-level state, not individual stabilising tokens.

## Passage-level spectrum

The enrichment fraction over a window determines the character of the output:

| Enrichment fraction | Character |
|---------------------|-----------|
| ~80%+ | Noise — no structure to absorb novelty |
| ~15-25% | Productive — minority enriching within stabilising scaffold |
| ~2-5% | Suppressed — narrowing, on the slope toward degeneration |
| ~0% | Degenerate — total self-prediction, fixed point |

Whether the paper names all four or just anchors the two extremes and the productive zone is TBD. The fine-grained taxonomy may belong in the experimental work (autoloop).

## Changes to §3.4

1. **Rename section**: "Compressive novelty and the enrichment fraction" (or similar — current title "The enriching and degrading regimes" implies the binary framing we're dropping).

2. **Drop the opening framing** that positions human-contributed context as the gold standard. The enrichment fraction applies symmetrically to any token stream received by any system with a compressive model. Human→model, model→human, model→model. Poorly constructed human input has a low enrichment fraction too.

3. **Introduce enrichment fraction as the central variable** from the start. The two "limiting cases" (current lines 54-56) become anchor points on a continuum, not regimes.

4. **Rename per-token labels**: degrading → stabilising for the healthy case, degenerate for the pathological extreme. The insight: ~80% of tokens in coherent prose are stabilising, and that's not a problem — it's what makes the enriching tokens legible.

5. **Forward reference to §4**: note that because the projection bottleneck is substrate-independent, the enrichment fraction applies to any sequential communication through a bandwidth-limited channel. This is a claim that §4 will develop, not prove here.

6. **Figures stay as-is** — they already demonstrate the spectrum. Captions may need minor updates to use the new vocabulary.
